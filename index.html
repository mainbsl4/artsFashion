Company Profile

NOMINEE DESIGN one of the leading Buying agent,manufacture and exporter of readymade garments in Bangladesh managed by a team of professionals engaged in the business for more then a decade have gained immense reputation at home and abrode.Our main focus is offering outstanding products to our customers and sticking to timely delivery schedules.


Our policy

Our policy

We try and make sure that our customers are fully satisfied with our performance and results.To ensure this we offer impeccable services in the arena of sampling production,quality,shipment schedules and the best communication at all times.We are potentially selling and sourcing high quality readymade garments in bulk volume at competitive prices.
